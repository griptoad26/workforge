How to set up sync

  1. Run cluster-hub on your desktop or NASPython 3 script, listens on port 8090 by default. The script is open source at github.com/griptoad26/xkg-desktop.
  2. Make the hub reachable from your iPhoneEither same Wi-Fi, or Tailscale/ZeroTier for cross-network. The mobile app needs to reach the hub at http://<hub-ip>:8090.
  3. Open XKG Mobile → Settings → HubEnter the hub URL. The app will fetch its config and start syncing.
  4. Capture on desktopPress Ctrl+Shift+X. Within 5 seconds, the capture appears in the iOS app's conversation list.

What's in the current beta

  • Read-only browse of captures from your desktop
  • Full-text search across all conversations
  • Launch a new chat with any of 4 providers (Grok, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini)
  • JSONL bridge auto-discovery on the local network
  • Encrypted API key storage in iOS Keychain

Not in this build: writing new captures from iOS, push notifications, Siri integration, iPad split-view. Those are coming in v0.2.0 / v0.3.0.
